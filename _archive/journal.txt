═══ WHY THIS BATCH EXISTED ═══

The 2-consecutive-loss halt fired on two consecutive days and blocked
12+ hours of data collection each time:
  - Sunday Apr 19, 21:37 ET — after BTC SHORT + SOL SHORT losses
  - Monday Apr 20, 17:14 ET — after BTC LONG loss

Bayesian math: at ~30% win rate, P(2 consecutive losses) = 49%. The
halt was firing on the modal outcome of any two-trade day. Live Railway
logs at 20:17 ET Apr 20 confirmed the halt was actively rejecting
bullish BTC signals while BTC had aligned trend + HTF bias.

Wayne's stated philosophy: "log everything, block nothing (except
Topstep-required), collect data on would-have-been-blocked signals
to decide which gates deserve to come back based on evidence."

═══ HOW SHADOW OUTCOME TRACKING WORKS ═══

`check_missed_setups()` in strategy_log.py runs every scan cycle.
For every strategy_log.csv row NOT marked as FIRED (and WITH target
+ stop values), it checks live candle HIGH/LOW since the row's
timestamp:
  - If price hit target → result = "WOULD_WIN"
  - If price hit stop → result = "WOULD_LOSE"
  - Otherwise unchanged (still tracking)

Uses candle ranges (not just close) so spikes between scans are caught.

Setup-level SHADOW rows (SHADOW_COOLDOWN, SHADOW_FAMILY_CD,
SHADOW_ZONE_LOCK, SHADOW_HALTED, REJECTED_SUSPENDED) have target + stop
and get outcome-tracked automatically.

Scan-level SHADOW rows (SHADOW_PROFIT_LOCK, SHADOW_MAX_TRADES,
SHADOW_CORRELATION, SHADOW_MARKET_HALT) have target=0 and are correctly
skipped by check_missed_setups — they represent "old gate would have
blocked THIS SCAN" rather than specific trades.

Daily recap shows today's shadow totals. Weekly recap aggregates 7 days
and recommends which gates to re-add (if any).

═══ KNOWN UNFIXED ISSUES (flagged for future batches) ═══

  - regime_classifier.py uses df['close'] vs bot.py's df['Close']
    (case mismatch → silently returns NEUTRAL via except clause)
    Impact: HTF regime filter effectively not working.
  - 15m crypto stops ~0.3% (1 ATR) — should be 1.8-2.0 ATR to survive
    normal noise. Sunday's BTC/SOL losses were stop-outs from noise,
    not direction failure.
  - max_contracts_NQ = 10 in sim_account.json — too loose for $50k eval
    (1 NQ × 20pt = $400 risk; 10 NQ × 20pt = $4000 = instant bust).
  - Conviction score may not be predictive (93/95/100 conviction trades
    have lost). Needs quintile IC analysis with ≥100 trade sample.
  - SOL futures NOT on Topstep permitted list. Full BTC also not
    permitted (only MBT Micro Bitcoin). Half the signal universe is
    untradable at target venue.
